Amenities

Party Hall Guidelines

Booking & Availability

  • Residents can book the Party Hall based on availability and applicable charges.
  • Each hall is equipped with 50 chairs. If only one party is scheduled during a particular slot, an additional 50 chairs can be provided upon request.

Cooking & Fire Safety

  • The use of LPG cylinders for frying or cooking is strictly prohibited in the Party Hall and clubhouse kitchens due to fire safety regulations.
  • Tandoors are not allowed in Party Halls.
  • Cooking is NOT permitted in the Party Hall.

Decoration Guidelines

  • Double-sided tape, drilling, or hammering nails for backdrops or stage decorations is not allowed.
  • Residents must use the existing hooks and nails provided.
  • Stage and hall decorations must be set up without damaging walls and can be done only 2 hours before the party start time.

Garbage Disposal

  • All waste must be packed in garbage disposal bags.
  • Housekeeping staff will collect the waste at the end of the event.

Time Extension & Charges

  • Party Hall usage beyond 4 hours will incur an additional charge for a minimum of 4 extra hours.

Water Supply

  • If the guest count exceeds 100, there may be delays in refilling overhead water tanks.

Vehicle Parking & Loading

  • Goods vehicles serving the event must be parked outside Gates A & B.
  • Loading/unloading is permitted near the clubhouse for a maximum of 20 minutes.
  • The clubhouse lift cannot be used for transporting heavy equipment. Such items must be carried via stairs.

Damage & Security Deposit

  • Any damage to Party Hall assets or clubhouse property will be deducted from the security deposit. The remaining balance will be refunded after necessary adjustments.

Guest Parking

  • The clubhouse does not have a designated parking area.
  • Guests and residents are advised to arrange drop-offs at the clubhouse and park vehicles outside Gates A & B.

Swimming Pool Usage Guidelines

Maintenance

  • All Mondays – Closed.
  • Note: All Tuesdays / Wednesdays / Thursdays / Saturdays / Sundays: Will be closed for maintenance from 08:00 AM to 09:00 AM.
  • On all Fridays, it will be closed from 06:00 AM to 10:00 AM.

Compliance & Safety

  • All users must follow the instructions of the pool attendant at all times. Emergency procedures must be strictly observed.
  • No Running: Walk, do not run, in and around the pool area to prevent accidents.
  • Supervision of Children: Children must always be accompanied and supervised by a parent or guardian in the pool and deck area.

Hygiene & Cleanliness

  • Maintain strict hygiene. Spitting, spouting water, or blowing the nose in the pool is strictly prohibited. Please use designated toilets for such needs.

Proper Attire

  • Gents: Only proper swimming trunks are allowed.
  • Ladies: Only proper swimsuits are allowed.
  • No casual clothing (e.g., jeans, t-shirts) in the pool.

Other Restrictions

  • No Pets: Pets are not allowed in the pool area.
  • Prohibited Substances: Persons under the influence of alcohol or narcotics will not be allowed in the pool area.
  • Food & Beverages: Food, beverages, and alcoholic drinks are not permitted in the pool area.
  • Floatation Devices: Use of floatation devices (e.g., tubes, arm floats) must be approved by the pool attendant.
  • Sunscreen & Showering: Patrons are encouraged to use sunscreen. Showering before entering and after applying sunscreen is mandatory.
  • Health Restrictions: Persons with open wounds, cuts, sores, bandages, or any infectious ailments are strictly prohibited from entering the pool.
  • Declaration of Swimming Ability: All patrons must inform the pool attendant of their swimming ability, experience, and skill level.
  • Avoid Distracting Security: Security guards must not be engaged in unnecessary conversations while on duty.
  • Restricted Areas: No sunbathing, sitting, or lounging IN the pool’s edge.
  • Enforcement of Rules: Users violating any rules may be asked to leave the pool and pool area immediately.

Ladies Batch Timings

  • All days from 3:30 PM to 5:50 PM (Except Mondays).

Gym Usage Guidelines

Timings

  • Morning: 5:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Access & Logbook

  • Entry is allowed only with a valid Amenities ID card.
  • Members must record their entry and exit times in the logbook.

Age Restriction

  • The gym is open to individuals 16 years and above. Those under 18 require parental consent.

Footwear

  • Only proper gym shoes are allowed. No sandals, boots, slippers, open-toed shoes, or bare feet.
  • Outside footwear is strictly prohibited inside the gym.

Equipment Usage & Care

  • Cardio Equipment: Usage is limited to 15 minutes during peak hours.
  • Weights Handling: Do not drop, slam, or throw weights on the floor.
  • Hygiene: Wipe down machines and equipment after use.
  • Equipment Usage: Return weights and equipment to their designated places after use.
  • Damaged Equipment: Report any malfunctioning or damaged equipment to management and do not use it.

Conduct & Responsibility

  • Respect Others: Maintain personal space. Use headphones for music. Keep phone conversations quiet.
  • Equipment Care: Damaging gym equipment will result in disciplinary action and fines.
  • Food & Beverages: No food is allowed inside the gym.
  • Personal Belongings: Management is not responsible for any lost or stolen items.
  • Theft: Any act of theft will result in blacklisting and strict action by management.
  • Ventilation & AC: Members are requested to keep windows and doors closed and use the air conditioning.

Important Notice

  • Outsiders are strictly prohibited from using the gym. Violators will face strict action.
  • Management is not responsible for any injuries sustained while using gym facilities.
  • Timings will be reviewed periodically by the Estate Management on the advice of the Executive Committee.

Children's Play Area – Usage Guidelines

  • The play area is strictly for children under the age of 12.
  • Throwing sand on play equipment or footpaths is prohibited.
  • Pets are not allowed in the play area.
  • Littering is strictly prohibited. Please use designated bins.
  • Play equipment must be used appropriately and as intended.
  • Running, pushing, or shoving is not allowed.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited.
  • Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times.
  • The toddler swing is reserved for children up to 4 years of age.

Note

  • Strict action will be taken against unauthorized individuals using the amenities.
  • The management is not responsible for any injuries sustained while using these facilities.

Guidelines for Usage of Pushpak Viman (Buggy)

Eligibility & Booking

  • Eligibility: Pushpak Viman is exclusively for registered senior citizens and differently abled persons of BM Phase 1.
  • Ticket Requirement: Tickets are mandatory for using the buggy service.
  • Ticket Distribution: Tickets are available only through the Senior Citizens Forum.

Operating Schedule & Pickups

  • Operating Schedule: The service operates as per the published time schedule.
  • Designated Pickup Points: Pickups are strictly from designated locations only.

Capacity & Baggage

  • Passenger Limit: Each ride can accommodate a maximum of 5 passengers.
  • Baggage Restriction: Only handbags or grocery bags up to 7 kg are allowed. Trolley bags and large luggage are not permitted.

Seating & Boarding

  • Seating Policy: Reservations are not allowed. Seating is strictly on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Boarding & Deboarding: Passengers must exercise caution while boarding and exiting the vehicle.

Fare Policy

  • Fare Policy: Ticket pricing will be reviewed periodically by the Senior Citizens Forum to ensure proper maintenance of the vehicle.

Note

  • The management is not responsible for any injuries sustained while using these facilities.
