Rule Violation & Penalty

Rule Violation & Penalty

Community Conduct Guidelines

For the safety, security, and well-being of the residential community, it is essential that all residents and visitors adhere to the established rules and regulations. Security personnel have the right to intervene and validate residents' details if necessary. We encourage everyone to follow these guidelines to ensure a harmonious living environment.

Activities That May Invite Penalties

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited in public areas, including balconies. The entire residential complex is a No-Smoking Zone.
  • Consumption of alcohol is not permitted in public places, including terraces.
  • Walking a pet without a leash.
  • Walking a pet without carrying a poop scooper.
  • Using vehicles with altered silencers.
  • Unauthorized vehicle entry into the society premises.
  • Unauthorized parking in designated or non-parking areas.
  • Using abusive language in public spaces, especially in common amenities.
  • Entering through a No Entry route.
  • Damaging landscaping, trees, plants, flowers, or green areas.
  • Honking after 10:00 PM without a valid reason.
  • Improper garbage disposal, including mixing waste without proper segregation.
  • Playing loud music or hosting parties that cause disturbance to neighbors.
  • Gathering in public areas after 10:00 PM without prior approval or creating a nuisance.
  • Rash driving within the society premises; speed limit restricted to 15 km/hr.
  • Tenant restrictions:
    • Maximum number of bachelor tenants:
    • A Block - 1 tenant
    • B Block - 2 tenants
    • C Block - 3 tenants
  • No visitors allowed without prior approval.
  • Security has the authority to inspect at any time based on complaints or patrol reports.
  • Unauthorized interior work on holidays, Sundays, or after 6:00 PM on other days.
  • Throwing waste in common areas or public spaces.
  • Move-in or move-out restrictions: Not allowed on holidays, Sundays, or after 6:00 PM on other days.
  • Violation of specific facility rules, such as swimming pool or sports amenities.
  • Using passenger lifts for heavy items or move-in/move-out activities.

Actions That Invite Heavy Penalties

  • Physical assault or manhandling (Legal action will also be taken).
  • Causing damage to society facilities (e.g., lifts, gym, swimming pool, buggy, or any infrastructure).
  • Sexual harassment or abuse of minors (Immediate legal action will be taken).
  • Accidents caused due to rule violations.
  • Property damage due to non-compliance with rules.
  • Use or possession of drugs or any related illegal activity.
  • Supporting or engaging in criminal activities.
  • Excessive water wastage, such as keeping taps open for extended periods.
  • Residing without proper legal documents.
  • Unauthorized commercial activities using society amenities or infrastructure.
  • Spitting inside lifts, lobbies, or other public areas.
